How 2-Step Authentication Works

  1. Enter your password normally.
  2. A code is sent to your phone either through text or voice message. Only someone with your phone can access it.

Why use it?

Two-step verification reduces the risk that somebody will access your account and personal information because they need your phone to overcome the second step. Even if someone has your username and password, they won't be able to sign in.
